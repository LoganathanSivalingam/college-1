Who we are

Sri Krishna Adithya College of Arts and Science (SKACAS), under the fold of Sri Krishna Institutions (since 1980), was established in May 2015 with the motto ‘Grooming Leaders for the Twenty-first Century’. The Institution is affiliated to Bharathiar University, Coimbatore with UGC recognition under section 2(f) & 12(B) of the UGC Act 1956.

VISION

  • To impart vibrant learning environment, inspiring innovation and nurturing creativity with technological competency grooming highly employable global citizens.

  • To provide excellent infrastructural and educational opportunities with integrity that help the students to excel holistically in academic and career endeavors

MISSION

  • To be the benchmark for elite education to confront global challenges

  • To nurture the students with the horizon of knowledge, attitudes, skills and practices for empowering the nation

  • To instill the recent trends in the upgrading technologies

  • To be the front runner in responsive research which fosters the idea of sustainable and eco-friendly environment

Smt. S. Malarvizhi, Chairperson and Managing Trustee of Sri Krishna Institutions, is an eminent educationalist inspiring the young minds with her rich academic experience and leads the college towards excellence in all spheres. She epitomizes women empowerment and the guiding force behind the awe inspiring growth that Sri Krishna Institutions have attained over the years. She was the Member of Syndicate of the prestigious Anna University which governs approximately 550 Engineering Colleges in the state of Tamilnadu during the period 2012 – 2015. She is a social worker, founder convener of Indian Women Network of Coimbatore Chapter of Confederation of Indian Industries (CII) and has been the Co-chair of a National Conference on Higher Education organized by CII held at Coimbatore. She is a Vice-President of Tamilnadu Athletic Association, Chennai and Member of Coimbatore District Sports Council. She is a recipient of EDUICON Award for the two consecutive years 2016 and 2017, constituted by Times of India Group, for her outstanding contributions in the field of education and Life Time Achievement Award 2017 for Education, awarded by ASSOCHAM- New Delhi and Lifetime Achiever Award 2018 by News7 Channel. She is also a Management Committee Member EPSI-New Delhi and Education Panel Member, Tamilnadu State Council, FICCI. Under her stewardship, over 4,000 students are placed in Multi-National Companies every year in Campus Recruitment. She is a “Leader with a Heart” and helps a lot for underprivileged children. Her ambition is ‘Every child should be imparted with proper education’.

Dr. K .Sundararaman M.Com., M.Phil., Ph.D. has 42 years of academic experience in various Institutions. He started his career as Assistant Professor of Commerce and elevated to the position of Head of the Department of Commerce in a leading Autonomous College in Coimbatore. He was also managing all Self – Financing streams in the College for 17 years. He joined Sri Krishna Arts and Science College as Principal in the year 2005 during his tenure the College achieved many laurels including ISO 2008, accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ grade and Autonomous status from UGC. He became the Chief Executive Officer of Sri Krishna Institutions in the year 2015. He has published five books. He has also published many research articles in Inter – national and National level journals. He is a member of Educational Panel of CII, Coimbatore region. He is also a member of Academic Council of many Autonomous Colleges in Bharathiar University. His expertise include Technology based teaching process, creating E – Learning platform and Outcome Based Education.

Dr.S.Palaniammal, Principal joined this prestigious Sri Krishna group of Institutions in 1986; has 38 years of rich teaching and research experience. Carrying the motto of the institution which is ‘Grooming Leaders for the Twenty-first Century’ Dr.S.Palaniammal has been empowering the student community for the last 38 years. She completed Master’s degree in Mathematics during the year 1986, M.Phil. in 1988, and Ph.D. in 2006 and started her career as a Lecturer in Mathematics with Sri Krishna Institutions where over the year she held various teaching as well as administrative responsibilities. Her hard work and perseverance have become the inspiration for many students and faculties in her journey as an educationist. A total of 17 research scholars have completed doctorates and 8 scholars are pursuing Ph.D. under her guidance; she published 172 papers in various National/International Journals and authored 8 books on Mathematics which are published by renowned publishers. Along with serving as a member of various expert committees, she is called to many institutions to share her expertise. She received grants from prominent organizations such as DRDO, CSIR, BRNS, MOES, and AICTE for the conduct of conferences and seminars. Dr.S.Palaniammal is recognized as the top 10 Best Women Leader from Education 2022 by Women Entrepreneur Magazine; South Indian Women Achiever Award 2019, Lifetime Education Achievement Award from National and International Compendium, New Delhi; Rotary Vocational Excellence Award 2019-2020; Certificate of Appreciation by Government Hospital for the support rendered during COVID pandemic and from Chandran’s Yuva Foundation in recognition for the extraordinary support for Book Donation Drive for setting up free libraries in Government Schools, Shield of Appreciation from Nirmala College for Women in appreciation of her academic prowess, International Institution Award 2016, 2017 and 2018 from BEC, Best Faculty Award twice from Sri Krishna Institutions. She has received Best Educationist Award and National Mahila Rattan Gold Medal Award from Dr. Bhishma Narain Singh, Former Central Minister and Former Governor of Tamil Nadu. She has also received Dr APJ Kalam’s Best Administrator-2017 Award from Dr Kalam educational trust, Chennai.
