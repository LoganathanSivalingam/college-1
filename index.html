Who we are

Sri Krishna Adithya College of Arts and Science (SKACAS), under the fold of Sri Krishna Institutions (since 1980), was established in May 2015 with the motto ‘Grooming Leaders for the Twenty-first Century’. The Institution is affiliated to Bharathiar University, Coimbatore with UGC recognition under section 2(f) & 12(B) of the UGC Act 1956.
SKACAS has been awarded with “Excellence in Arts and Science” in Kongu Region by News 18 Television Channel. This prestigious award has been conferred based on a survey conducted all over Tamil Nadu under various categories. The remarkable features of Sri Krishna Adithya CAS like Infrastructure, Digitalised Teaching-Learning, On-campus Professional, Government exams coaching and Add-on Courses, consistent University ranks and Gold Medals, Industry Expert Talks, illustrious Placement records, Clubs and Community outreach programmes fetched this award.
